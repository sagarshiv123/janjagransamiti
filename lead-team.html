Jan Jagaran Samiti (JJS) is a non-profit organization based in Pamgarh block of Janjgir-Champa district of Chhattisgarh. JJS works towards creating an equitable society by improving the living conditions of the poor and marginalised through community initiatives, participation and sensitization. The organization came into effect in 2007 with an aim to promote women empowerment

and enhance the livelihood and literacy status of the community. JJS encourages women self-help groups and small scale farmers to participate in all interventions and livelihood related activities, subsequently redirecting them to contribute towards the development process of the community. Vision: JJS seeks to build a progressive, self-reliant society by empowering women from the marginalized communities through various developmental initiatives. Mission: To envision a society based on liberty of choices, equality and universal brotherhood, where every person can claim their right to education, health, livelihood and social security.
